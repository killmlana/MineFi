Features

Everything you need

A complete payment solution built for Minecraft servers.

Instant Spending

Players deposit once, spend instantly in any shop. No wallet popups, no gas fees, no delays.

WalletConnect v2

Scan a QR code on an in-game map to link MetaMask, Rainbow, or any WC-compatible wallet.

Multi-Provider

Crypto, Stripe, and Razorpay out of the box. Enable any combination or add your own provider.

Vault Economy

Drop-in Vault provider. Works with EssentialsX, ShopGUI+, ChestShop, and every Vault-compatible plugin.

Smart Contract Escrow

EIP-712 meta-transaction withdrawals. Merkle proof balance verification. Emergency withdraw for safety.

Book GUI

All interaction through clickable in-game books. Players right-click a wallet item to start.

Setup

Three steps to payments

Drop in the JAR, configure your providers, and go.

1

Drop the JAR

Place MineFi.jar in your plugins folder alongside Vault. Restart the server.

2

Configure providers

Add your Stripe key, Razorpay credentials, or WalletConnect project ID in config.yml.

3

Players pay

Right-click the wallet book, pick a method, scan QR or click the link. Balance credited instantly.

providers:
  crypto:
    enabled: true
    walletconnect:
      project-id: "your-wc-project-id"
  stripe:
    enabled: true
    secret-key: "sk_live_..."
  razorpay:
    enabled: true
    key-id: "rzp_live_..."
    key-secret: "..."
